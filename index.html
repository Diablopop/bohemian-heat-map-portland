Bohemian Heat Map: Portland

This is a proof-of-concept to show how Portland residents and visitors could find neighborhoods that support "bohemian" lifestyles based on the proximity to art spaces, vegan restaurants, theaters, game stores and other interesting places.

The data about businesses is static and may not be updated. Additionally, some of the data—especially about vegan restaurants—is not very reliable, but could be improved with paid data sources.
